How to Use Prompt Engineer v2.0

1. Describe Your Tool

Tell the AI what kind of tool you want to create. Be specific about its purpose, target audience, and functionality.

2. Configure Fields

The AI will suggest fields based on your description. You can add, edit, or remove fields as needed. Each field can be configured with validation rules and permissions.

3. Set Permissions

Decide which fields should be premium. Free users will see these fields locked with upgrade prompts, creating natural monetization opportunities.

4. Deploy

Choose a subdomain and deploy your tool. It will be instantly available at your custom URL.

Examples of Tools You Can Create:

  • Health risk assessments and symptom checkers
  • Business analysis and planning tools
  • Story generators and creative writing assistants
  • Calculators and converters
  • Quizzes and educational assessments
  • Decision-making tools and recommendation systems
